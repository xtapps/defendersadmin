Acceptable Proof of Service (Open to US and Canada Defenders)

  • VA Healthcare ID Card
  • Veterans ID Card
  • Driver’s License with Veteran designator
  • DD Form 214 (Discharge from Service)
  • DD Form 215 (Discharge from Service – Corrected)
  • NGB Form 22 (Discharge from National Guard Service)
  • NGB Form 23 (Discharge from National Guard Service - Corrected)
  • DD Form 256A (Discharge Certificate)
  • VA Determination Letter
  • VA Certificate of Eligibility
  • DFAS Form 702 - Military Leave & Earnings Statement (L.E.S)
  • DFAS-CL 7720/148 – Military Retiree Account Statement
  • US Army Reserve / National Guard Military Retirement Points Statement
  • EMT/Paramedic Certification or ID Card with name and title
  • Firefighter certification or ID Card with name and title
  • Law Enforcement Officers certification or ID Card with name and title
  • 911 Dispatchers ceritication or ID Card with name and title
  • Retired First Responders retiree card or retirement certificate
  • Veteran's Service Card
  • CFOne Card
